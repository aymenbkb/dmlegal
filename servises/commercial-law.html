Commercial Law

Home › Services › Commercial Law

Also known as Business Law or Trade Law, is the branch of law that governs the rights, relationships, and conduct of individuals and businesses engaged in commerce, trade, and sales. It provides the legal framework for business operations and ensures fair and lawful practices in commercial activities.

Key Areas of Commercial Law Include:

  • Contracts: Rules for creating and enforcing agreements between parties.
  • Sales of Goods: Laws governing the buying and selling of goods and services.
  • Agency: Legal relationships where one party acts on behalf of another.
  • Banking and Finance: Regulations for financial transactions and institutions.
  • Intellectual Property: Protection of trademarks, patents, and copyrights.
  • International Trade: Rules for imports, exports, and cross-border transactions.
  • Dispute Resolution: Mechanisms for resolving conflicts in business dealings.
