Tax Law

Home › Services › Tax Law

At DM Legal, we provide expert guidance on all aspects of tax law to help businesses navigate the complexities of VAT registration, corporate tax registration, tax compliance, and cross-border tax issues. We handle everything from routine VAT filings to complex tax disputes, ensuring your business remains compliant and protected from unnecessary risks.

Key Areas of Our Tax Law Services Include:

  • VAT Registration: Assisting businesses with the registration process for VAT compliance in the UAE.
  • UAE Corporate Tax: Assisting individuals and companies in understanding the implementation of UAE Corporate Tax and minimising risk exposure in UAE and abroad.
  • Tax Compliance: Ensuring that businesses adhere to tax laws and regulations, minimizing the risk of penalties.
  • Income Tax Returns: Providing support in filing and managing income tax returns for businesses and individuals.
  • Cross-Border Taxation: Offering guidance on international tax issues, including tax treaties and transfer pricing.
  • Tax Disputes and Litigation: Representing clients in disputes with tax authorities and resolving complex tax issues.
  • Financial Management: Advising on accounting, financial reporting, and structuring tax-efficient business operations.
Our team of experts include correspondents in France, the UK, and Israel, providing specialized guidance on bilateral treaties, cross-border asset transfers, relocations, and compliance.
