Criminal Law

Home › Services › Criminal Law
⚖️

Our criminal law and compliance services in the UAE are designed to provide expert guidance and robust defense. With a deep understanding of the complexities of UAE criminal law and the Justice System, our team of seasoned lawyers ensures comprehensive support throughout every stage of your case.

Key Areas of Our Criminal Law Services:

  • ✓Criminal Defense: Representing clients in criminal cases to safeguard their rights, including extradition proceedings, Interpol Red Notices, and arrest warrant matters.
  • ✓Criminal Claim: Assisting victims of criminal offences and guiding them throughout the judicial process, from the claim to damages allocation.
  • ✓Legal Advice: Providing expert legal opinion regarding criminal matters to help clients understand their options.
  • ✓Case Management: Offering complete support throughout the legal process, from arrest to trial and sentencing.
  • ✓Appeals: Representing clients in appellate proceedings before the Court of First Instance, Court of Appeal, and the Supreme Court.
  • ✓Compliance: Ensuring compliance with UAE laws, regulations, and procedures throughout the criminal process.
At dmlegal-firm, we specialize in criminal law, offering expert defense strategies and legal advice in the UAE. We guide clients through every stage of the criminal justice process, ensuring that their rights are protected. All procedures are handled in collaboration with local attorneys representing clients before UAE Courts and abroad (ECHR, ICC The Hague).
