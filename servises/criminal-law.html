Criminal Law

Home › Services › Criminal Law

Our criminal law and compliance services are designed to provide expert guidance and robust defense across international jurisdictions. With a deep understanding of the complexities of criminal law systems worldwide, our team of seasoned lawyers ensures comprehensive support throughout every stage of your case. From sound legal advice to developing effective, tailored defense strategies, we are committed to protecting your rights and achieving the best possible outcomes in both domestic and international proceedings.

Key Areas of Our Criminal Law Services Include:

  • Criminal Defense: Representing clients in criminal cases to safeguard their rights and ensure a fair trial, including in extradition proceedings, Interpol Red Notices, and arrest warrant matters across multiple jurisdictions.
  • Interpol Red Notice Defense: Specialized expertise in challenging and managing Interpol Red Notices, including requests for removal, review, and defense against wrongful notifications.
  • Extradition Proceedings: Comprehensive representation in extradition cases, including defense against extradition requests and international surrender proceedings.
  • Legal Advice: Providing expert legal opinion regarding criminal matters to help clients understand their options across different legal systems.
  • Case Management: Offering complete support throughout the legal process, from investigation to trial and sentencing in both domestic and international courts.
  • Appeals: Representing clients in appellate proceedings before various courts, including national courts, international tribunals, and supranational courts to challenge unfair convictions or sentences.
  • International Cooperation: Managing cross-border legal matters, mutual legal assistance requests, and international criminal investigations.
At DM Legal, we specialize in criminal law with international scope, offering expert defense strategies and legal advice across multiple jurisdictions. Our experienced legal team is committed to providing robust representation in criminal cases worldwide. We guide clients through every stage of the criminal justice process, ensuring that their rights are protected and their best interests are upheld. All procedures are handled in collaboration with local attorneys representing clients in front of national courts and international tribunals (ECHR, ICC the Hague, Interpol, and other supranational bodies).
