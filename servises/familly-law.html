Family Law

Home › Services › Family Law

Family Law governs legal matters related to family, including but not limited to marriage, divorce, child custody, alimony, and inheritance. The UAE legal framework is primarily influenced by both civil law and Sharia principles, with distinct provisions for Muslims and non-Muslims.

Federal Law No. 28 of 2005

On Personal Status, as amended (Personal Status Law Federal Law No. 41 of 2024), governs personal affairs: marriage, dissolution of marriage, divorce, fostering, capacity, tutelage, and guardianship.

Key Areas of Family Law Include:

  • Divorce Proceedings: Legal processes for dissolving marriages and resolving related disputes.
  • Child Custody and Visitation: Determining parenting arrangements and visitation rights.
  • Alimony and Financial Support: Establishing financial support for spouses and dependents.
  • Inheritance and Wills: Drafting and execution of Wills and inheritance distribution.
Our team works closely with local correspondents who represent clients before UAE Courts and abroad, ensuring that all family-related legal matters, including divorce proceedings, legacy issues, and custody arrangements, are handled with the atmost care and sensitivity to both legal requirements and cultural nuances.
