Business Setup in UAE Free Zones

Home › Services › Business Setup in UAE Free Zones

Business establishment in the Free Zones of the UAE provides a streamlined framework for foreign investors to set up and operate companies. These zones offer 100% foreign ownership, tax exemptions, simplified registration processes, and access to world-class infrastructure. Businesses benefit from tailored regulations designed to encourage innovation, attract global talent, and foster economic growth in a competitive environment.

Key Benefits of Setting Up in UAE Free Zones Include:

  • 100% Foreign Ownership: Enjoy full control of your business without the need for a local sponsor.
  • Tax Exemptions: Benefit from exemptions on corporate taxes and personal income taxes for a set period.
  • Simplified Registration: Quick and easy company formation processes with minimal bureaucracy.
  • World-Class Infrastructure: Access to state-of-the-art facilities and business environments.
  • Tailored Regulations: Free Zones are designed to foster innovation, economic growth, and attract global talent.
  • Strategic Location: Situated in key locations offering access to international markets, especially in logistics and trade.
At DM Legal, we offer expert guidance for setting up businesses in the UAE's Free Zones. Whether you're looking to establish a new company, register your business, or ensure full legal compliance, our team provides tailored solutions. We offer in-depth knowledge of Free Zone regulations, tax benefits, and strategic advice on choosing the best Free Zone to suit your business needs.
