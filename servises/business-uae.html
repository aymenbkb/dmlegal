Business Setup in UAE Free Zones

Home › Services › Business Setup in UAE Free Zones

Business establishment in the Free Zones of the UAE provides a streamlined framework for foreign investors to set up and operate companies. These zones offer 100% foreign ownership, tax exemptions, simplified registration processes, and access to world-class infrastructure.

Key Benefits of Setting Up in UAE Free Zones:

  • Business Registration: Complete assistance with company formation and registration across UAE free zones.
  • License Acquisition: Securing appropriate business licenses for various commercial activities.
  • Legal Structure Advisory: Guidance on optimal business structures for different activities.
  • Documentation Support: Comprehensive assistance with all required documentation.
  • Ongoing Compliance: Support for maintaining regulatory compliance.
  • Strategic Location Planning: Expert advice on choosing optimal free zones.
At DM Legal, we offer expert guidance for setting up businesses in the UAE's Free Zones. Whether you're looking to establish a new company, register your business, or ensure full legal compliance, our team provides tailored solutions. We offer in-depth knowledge of Free Zone regulations, tax benefits, and strategic advice on choosing the best Free Zone to suit your business needs.
