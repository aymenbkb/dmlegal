Denis Acker

Advocate and Legal Consultant

Founding Partner, dmlegal-firm

Denis Acker, Founding Partner, is a Canadian Attorney and co-founder of the Legal Consultancy Firm operating out of Dubai. Denis is a member of the Quebec Bar Association with over twenty years of experience. He specializes in Company Formation, Establishment of Trusts and Foundations, as well as Investment Immigration. He offers guidance to foreign investors on their projects for establishment in Malta and other EU countries.

CREDENTIALS & ACHIEVEMENTS
Over 20 years of experience in corporate and investment law.
Successfully assisted numerous multinational corporations in setting up businesses in the UAE and EU.
Expert in trust formation and asset protection strategies.
Recognized for his work in investment immigration and international business structuring.
Regular speaker at international legal and business conferences.
