Jordana Dray

Advocate and Legal Consultant

Jordana Dray is an internationally accomplished legal expert specializing in cross-border matters and complex litigation. She has worked in Paris at Olivier Metzner LLP and D.O. Kaminski LLP, and in Israel with Meitar Liquvornik Geva Leshem and Professor Yuval Levy. In 2013, she founded her own boutique firm in Paris before joining the UAE practice. She leverages her global expertise to help individuals and companies navigate legal challenges between Europe and the Middle East. Fluent in French, English, and Hebrew.
